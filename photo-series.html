Project File #1 — Photography Series

Self-Portrait Photography Series

Inspired by my painting in Project #2

A series of staged self-portraits exploring ADHD, chronic illness, and the emotional weight of living with both. Each image focuses on a different feeling, symptom, or mental state, turning personal experience into visual symbols that are sometimes tense, sometimes quiet, and sometimes ironic. This series began as a way to translate inner experiences into images. It is built around moments that are difficult to explain in plain language: paralysis, overstimulation, fatigue, pain, dependence, repetition, and the constant push and pull between wanting to act and not being able to.

I used self-portrait images because the work is personal and direct. Rather than trying to make the series overly polished or overly literal, I let the images carry the feeling through pose, repetition, layering, symbolic objects, and atmosphere.
