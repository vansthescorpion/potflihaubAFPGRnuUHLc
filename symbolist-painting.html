Project File #2 — Mixed-media painting

Self-Portrait Painting

This mixed-media self-portrait brings together different materials and symbols to reflect the tension between creativity, illness, dependence, and emotional distance. The work combines acrylic paint with oil pastels, marker, reflective circles, and glued pills, making the piece feel both intimate and physically layered.

Concept: The painting explores what it feels like to carry both inner intensity and inner heaviness at the same time. It moves between energy and darkness, expression and numbness, showing how creativity, insomnia, medication, and fragility can all coexist in one body and one identity.

Gallery Exhibition Log — Stadthaus

The painting was also shown at Stadthaus, where seeing it in the gallery felt especially meaningful. A little girl looked up at it and said it was pretty, and that small reaction stayed with me because it felt honest, immediate, and unexpectedly affirming.
